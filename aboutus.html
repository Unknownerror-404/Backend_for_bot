🧠 What is AarogyaAI?

Our project, AarogyaAI, is an AI-based health chatbot designed to give users quick and trustworthy responses to basic medical queries. It acts as a virtual first-aid assistant that provides information about common symptoms, diseases, and health precautions.

In rural and semi-urban areas, people often don’t have quick access to medical consultation. They either ignore their symptoms or rely on unverified internet sources. AarogyaAI is our solution to bridge that gap by providing basic healthcare awareness instantly, anytime, anywhere.

⚙️ Technical Stack & Features

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
  • Backend: Python, Yaml, Rasa, Fireworks API.
  • AI/Chatbot: Rule-based logic & basic NLP using Python.
  • Database : Mongo DB, SQL.

The chatbot is built in a simple interface so even non-tech users can easily use it. It responds to keyword-based health-related queries like “fever,” “cold,” “headache,” “stomach pain,” etc., and provides relevant guidance.
